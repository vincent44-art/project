Welcome to the Product Inventory web application

Managing your inventory has never been easier! Our Product Inventory web application is designed to help you keep track of your products efficiently. Whether you're a small business owner, a warehouse manager, or just someone who loves staying organized, this application is here to simplify your life.

Key Features

  • Add Products: Easily add new products to your inventory by providing the product name, quantity, and price.
  • View Products: See a complete list of all your products, including their names, quantities, and prices.
  • Delete Products: Remove products from your inventory with just one click.
  • Calculate Total Cost: The system automatically calculates the total cost of all products in your inventory, giving you a clear overview of your stock value.

How It Works

  1. Add Products: Go to the Add Product page and fill out the form to add a new product to your inventory.
  2. View Products: Visit the View Products page to see all your products, their details, and the total cost of your inventory.
  3. Delete Products: On the View Products page, click the Delete button next to any product to remove it from your inventory.

Why Use This web application?

Our Product Inventory System is designed with simplicity and efficiency in mind. It helps you:

  • Keep track of your stock levels in real-time.
  • Calculate the total value of your inventory instantly.
  • Save time by managing your products in one place.
  • Stay organized and make informed decisions about your inventory.
